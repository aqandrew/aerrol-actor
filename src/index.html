Bio

Aerrol Ampeloquio is an actor, writer, and director based in Los Angeles, California. He has released a PSA on gender issues and two short films. His directorial debut is "Forgetting You", a short film in which he plays a man coping with a breakup from a toxic relationship. He currently attends the Conservatory program at The Second City: Hollywood and interns at the Studio for Performing Arts in Los Angeles.

Aerrol's goal in the world of entertainment is to create complex characters that challenge audiences' views by allowing them to look at life from different perspectives.
